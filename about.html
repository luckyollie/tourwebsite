About SingaporeExplorer

Your trusted local guide to discovering the real Singapore

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate Singaporeans, SingaporeExplorer was born from a desire to share our beautiful city with the world. We believe that the best way to experience Singapore is through the eyes of locals who truly love their home.

Our team of expert guides are not just tour guides - they're storytellers, historians, and passionate ambassadors of Singapore culture. Each tour is crafted to showcase the authentic side of Singapore, from hidden gems to iconic landmarks.
